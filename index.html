About me
Hello! My name is Mariya Ivanova, I am a photographer and videographer from St. Petersburg. From childhood she was interested in photography and composition, later she turned her hobby into a profession
I have been doing photography and videography for over 10 years. With my photographs, I do not just show beautiful pictures, but I tell stories and convey emotions caught at the moment of shooting

Work steps
1.Know you better
It is important for me to get to know you better and tell you about myself, so that you feel comfortable during the shooting, you can relax and enjoy the process
2. Concept
We will discuss all the details of the shooting, determine the concept, image and location. I will select references so that you can clearly see the result of the future shooting
3.Shooting
In the process of shooting, I give you all the recommendations for posing, we also change the location if necessary
4. Photos
In a week you will receive finished photos, original processed in author's retouching
